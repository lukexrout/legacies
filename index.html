About Us

Legacies Realty is an asset management company that provides professional Residential, Commercial, Short-Term, Industrial, and Real Estate services. We make sure to include the best technology that allows for our owners to make sure their assets are showing great return with little to no time investment, whilst our time is set aside for our tenants when they place maintenance requests and work orders.

Our main office is located locally in Loveland, Ohio.
We are independently owned and operated.

Our business has been going strong for the past 7 years. The experience and trust Legacies Realty has grown to have in Ohio truly reflects our service offerings and our ability to rise to the occasion.
